About

The Short Version

I spent 5 years in Shayetet 13 (Israeli Naval Special Forces) as a combat diver, sniper, and team leader. After military service, I taught myself to code, scored 100/100 in an intensive full-stack program, and started building things.

Since then I've founded a 45,000-member community, built a real estate tech platform from scratch, shipped 10+ web applications, completed a PM internship at a startup, and studied Business & CS at Reichman University (GPA 90). I also do standup comedy, write rap, and speak four languages.

I'm not a specialist. I'm the person you put on a problem when you need it solved — whatever it takes.
